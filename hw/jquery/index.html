Dope Art In My House

My family has been collecting art for years. Ever since high school, I have been going to art shows with my parents all over the country. Over the years my love and appreciation for art has grown and turned into a passion. Within this website are some of my favorite pieces that my family has collected, there is also a list of some of my favorite artist right now! Hope you enjoy.

Kaws
@kaws

I have been a huge fan of Kaws’ work ever since I started observing and collecting art. Kaws has been in the game for years and has shaped the world of art and hype beast culture. Over the years Kaws has done numerous collaborations and sculptures featured around the world. While many say Kaws has become “mainstream” recently due to his art’s accessibility, many of his original acrylic paintings have sold for six figures.

Bean in Soup
Ketnipz
@ketnipz

This is one of my Mom's favorite artist. We have various Ketnipz pieces throughout the house as well, but this is the first piece we bought. Ketnipz is one of youngest and most successful artist on Instagram artist right now. After blowing up from a random 9Gag post, Ketnipz has reached over a million followers on Instagram. You need to check him out if you want to smile!

Blast
Matthias Schmid

This is the most recent addition to our collection. We got this piece from Context Miami during Art Basel earlier this fall in 2019. This piece is titled "Blast" and uses various cut outs from comic books to create this masterpiece. I like this one a lot because I believe it transitions so many different generations and mediums into one solid piece.

Frank Ocean "Nights"
Rhymezlikedimez
@rhymezlikedimez

This piece holds a special place in my heart because Frank Ocean is my favorite music artist. It was painted by Robin Velghe, also known as Rhymezlikedimez, and this was one of his first acrylic paintings. I was fortunate to get to own one of his first acrylic pieces. Robin has done work with and for several famous hip-hop artist, and his work speaks for itself. Check him out on Instagram too!

Cannabis
This is Addictive
@thisisaddictive

This is one of my favorite pieces in the house. I got it for my 20th birthday from my mother and this piece was showcasing in "The Art Plug's Gallery" from Art Palm Beach 2018. I love this piece because it is a fun play on nutritional facts. We own a few pieces from him, but this is the first piece and dopest.
