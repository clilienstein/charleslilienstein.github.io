Assortment by Kaws

I have been a huge fan of Kaws ever since I started observing and collecting art. The pieces on the outside are titled "Along The Way" and the one in the middle is titled "Seeing/Watching". Over the years Kaws has done numerous collaborations and sculptures featured around the world.

Bean in Soup byKetnipz

This is my Mom's favorite artist. We have various Ketnipz pieces throughout the house as well. Ketnipz is one of youngest and most succesful artist on Instagram right now.

Blast by Matthias Schmid

This is the most recent addition to our collection. We got this piece from Context Miami during Art Basel earlier this fall in 2019. This piece is titled "Blast" and uses cut outs from comic books and was created by Matthias Schmid.

Frank Ocean "Nights" by Rhymezlikedimez

This piece holds a special place in my heart because Frank Ocean is my favorite music artist. It was painted by Robin Velghe, also known as Rhymezlikedimez, and this was one of his first acrylic paintings.

Cannabis by This is Addictive

This is one of my favorite pieces in the house. I got it for my 20th birthday from my mother and it was showcasing in "The Art Plug's Gallery" from Art Palm Beach.
